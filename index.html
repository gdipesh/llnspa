About Us

The restaurant features a diverse menu of Asian and international cuisine, expertly prepared by our skilled chefs using the freshest ingredients. Our cozy cafe offers a range of delicious pastries, sandwiches, and beverages, perfect for a quick bite or a leisurely afternoon of relaxation.

Spa

At Lotus Lounge and Spa Pvt Ltd, we offer a wide range of spa services that are designed to relax, rejuvenate, and revitalize your body and mind. Our team of skilled therapists and estheticians are dedicated to providing you with the best possible spa experience. From our signature massages and body treatments to our luxurious facials and manicures, we have something for everyone. Our peaceful and relaxing atmosphere, combined with our state-of-the-art facilities and top-quality products, make us the best choice for anyone looking to indulge in some much-needed pampering.

Live Music

Friday nights at Lotus Lounge and Spa are always a hit with our guests. The atmosphere is lively and vibrant as we feature live music performances by talented local artists. As you enjoy the music, you can sip on a refreshing cocktail or coffee from our bar and relax on our comfortable seating. The atmosphere is perfect for a romantic date or a night out with friends. So come and join us on a Friday night at Lotus Lounge and Spa for an unforgettable experience.

Best Lounge Experience

we strive to provide our guests with the best lounge experience possible. Our comfortable and stylish seating, combined with our wide selection of drinks and snacks from the bar, create the perfect atmosphere for relaxation and socializing. Whether you're looking to unwind after a long day or simply want to spend some quality time with friends and loved ones, our lounge is the perfect place to be. So come and join us and experience the best lounge in town.
